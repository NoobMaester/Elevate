Who we are

We are a Kigali-based software consultation company with a knack for quality. We have been in the business for over 6 years, we have worked with a multitude of companies and helped them achieve their goals through well-engineered software systems that just work and meet demands.

What We Do

Full-featured software projects

We will spearhead your project from start to finish. Essentially, our gifted designers with a keen eye on quality will make sure your project looks truly beautiful and professional. Then, our talented backend, frontend and mobile engineers will work on your project, using industry standards and best practices to bring your project to life.

Web Based Systems

Maybe you have a project that requires a web system and you need a strong team to bring it to life. We are here to make that dream a reality, with proven experience building high quality, enterprise grade web systems. From simple single page applications to complex backend systems which scale perfectly, we have you covered.

Mobile Apps

The world is getting more and more mobile, as a result, we live and breathe mobile, we are very much into mobile, call us mobile enthusiasts. We produce high quality, performant and visually pleasing mobile apps that can run on both Android and iOS. Additionally, by leveraging native app development, our apps are snappy and lightweight, ensuring a smooth experience across the board.
